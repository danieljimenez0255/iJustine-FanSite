iJustine Bio

  • Justine Ezarik (born March 20, 1984 [age 35]), better known online as iJustine or IJ, is an American YouTube
    vlogger and Actor who was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. She has appeared on many shows such as Law and
    Order: SVU and Criminal Minds.
  • She is the daughter of a p.e. teacher and a coal miner, She is the oldest sister with Breanna being the second
    oldest sister and Jenna being the youngest teacher
  • She graduated from Pittsburgh Technical Institute in 2004 where she landed several jobs in graphic design and
    video editing before she would soon start her Youtube channel on May 6, 2006.
  • Ezarik appeared live on the Internet through the use of a wireless webcam and microphone on her own iJustine
    lifecasting channel on Justin Kan's Justin.tv, where she began transmitting her life via the Internet on May 29, 2007.
  • When the iPhone debuted in June 2007, Ezarik covered the device's debut at the Mall of America in Bloomington,
    Minnesota a suburb south of Minneapolis, Minnesota.
  • In 2017, she released her first official book. In March 2019, she surpassed 1,000,000,000 views on her iJustine YouTube channel. She has also been nomminated for many awards such as for Best Vlogger Award(Streamy's), the Choice Web Star(Teen Choice Awards) and etc. But she has won an Webby Award for Web Personality/Host in 2011.
